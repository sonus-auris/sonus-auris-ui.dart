Sonus Auris is a personal "audio dashcam": it keeps a continuous, rolling
recording of your own surroundings so the moments that matter aren't lost — the
idea you mumble while driving, a song you want to identify, or the sounds of your
sleep. You start and stop capture; you decide what is kept.

YOUR AUDIO, ENCRYPTED ON YOUR DEVICE
• Every recording is encrypted on the device with AES-256-GCM before it is
  stored or uploaded. Keys stay on your device.
• Optional backup to storage you control — your own S3-compatible bucket,
  Google Drive, OneDrive, or iCloud. There is no shared Sonus Auris media server.
• Keep only a rolling window on the phone; older clips age out automatically.

USEFUL THINGS IT HEARS (ALL OPTIONAL, OFF BY DEFAULT)
• Sleep & snore insights from on-device audio analysis.
• Music recognition to label songs that play around you.
• Sound "commotion" alerts for sudden loud events while you record.
• An optional listening link you can email yourself around a flagged moment.

BUILT FOR PRIVACY
• Microphone access is used only after you start capture.
• Location tagging is optional, off by default, and only used to stamp where a
  clip was recorded if you turn it on.
• Delete any clip, or your whole account and its data, at any time.

PLEASE RECORD RESPONSIBLY
Audio recording and consent laws vary by country, state, and situation. You are
responsible for complying with the laws that apply to you and for obtaining
consent where it is required. Sonus Auris is intended for recording your own
environment, not for covertly recording other people.

Questions, privacy policy, and account deletion: see the in-app Settings and our
website.
