Sonus Auris is a personal "audio dashcam." It keeps a continuous, rolling
recording of your own surroundings so the moments that matter aren't lost — an
idea you mumble while driving, a song you want to identify, or the sounds of your
sleep. You start and stop capture, and you decide what is kept.

YOUR AUDIO, ENCRYPTED ON YOUR DEVICE
- Every recording is encrypted on the device with AES-256-GCM before it is stored
  or uploaded. Keys stay on your device.
- Optional backup to storage you control — your own S3-compatible bucket, Google
  Drive, OneDrive, or iCloud. There is no shared Sonus Auris media server.
- Keep only a rolling window on the phone; older clips age out automatically.

USEFUL THINGS IT HEARS (ALL OPTIONAL, OFF BY DEFAULT)
- Sleep and snore insights from on-device audio analysis.
- Music recognition to label songs that play around you.
- Sound alerts for sudden loud events while you record.

BUILT FOR PRIVACY
- The microphone is used only after you start capture.
- Location tagging is optional, off by default, and only stamps where a clip was
  recorded if you turn it on.
- Delete any clip, or your entire account and its data, at any time.

PLEASE RECORD RESPONSIBLY
Audio recording and consent laws vary by country, state, and situation. You are
responsible for complying with the laws that apply to you and for obtaining
consent where required. Sonus Auris is for recording your own environment, not
for covertly recording other people.
